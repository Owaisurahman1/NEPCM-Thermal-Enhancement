# 🔥 Major Project: Nano-Enhanced Phase Change Material (NEPCM)

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on improving the thermal conductivity of paraffin wax by adding copper and graphene nanoparticles.

## 🎯 Objective

* Enhance heat transfer rate
* Improve energy storage performance
* Increase efficiency of Phase Change Materials (PCM)

## 🧪 Materials Used

* Paraffin Wax (Base PCM)
* Copper Nanoparticles (Cu)
* Graphene Nanoparticles

## ⚙️ Methodology

* Preparation of nano-enhanced PCM (NEPCM)
* Uniform mixing of nanoparticles into paraffin wax
* Experimental testing of thermal performance
* Comparison with pure PCM

## 📊 Results

* Increased thermal conductivity
* Faster heat transfer
* Improved thermal energy storage efficiency

## 🔬 Applications

* Thermal energy storage systems
* Solar energy systems
* Cooling systems

## 🚀 Future Scope

* DSC analysis for latent heat
* Optimization of nanoparticle concentration
* Industrial-scale applications
